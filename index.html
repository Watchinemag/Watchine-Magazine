DISPOSITION

The current systems that be - captial, extraction and unsatiable comsumption - have merged money, work, labor and fulfillment into an inseparable beast. This condition has, in its entirety, inverted the relationship between valuation and quality. Paying more to get less. Ozempic economy


  • ( ╹ -╹)? Is all work meant to be monetized?

  • ( ╹ -╹)? Can labor be fulfillingly rewarded outside of the dollar?

  • ( ╹ -╹)? What can we gain, that is not money, from the many forms of "good work" and "hard labor"?

  • ( ╹ -╹)? How do we re-attribute quality in full seriousness and respect without monetary validation?

The newspaper doesn't know these answers. In spite of it all, Watchine Magazine labors to identify and exist within this carpet-y frictioned polycule of time, work, input/output and free-ness.

THIS EXISTENTIAL CONDITION PRODUCES THE FOLLOWING ETHIC:

  • Watchine Magazine always takes its play seriously.
  • Watchine Magazine is questioning and exploring the effectivity of collectivist input/output publishing.
  • Watchine Magazine is not paid to endorse or push a message. It projects whatever the hell it wants to with no barriers.
  • Watchine Magazine does not abide by random "terms and conditions".
  • Watchine Magazine refutes the way of being that every financial choice needs to be an investment.
  • Watchine Magazine does not abide the dollar. The dollar does not energize the labor. The labor is energized by the self, community and obsession to produce.
  • Watchine Magazine is tired of no integrity.
  • Watchine Magazine is sworn in full commitment to this manifesto. Any diversion from this foundation ceases "Watchine Magazine".
  • Watchine Magazine will NEVER sell its soul.
